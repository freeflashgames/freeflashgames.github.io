Primary Category: Action.

Game Description:

Your task is to collect all eggs and put them into correct baskets - railway carriages. BobiBobi moves in the choo-choo. Every carriage can contain up to 5 eggs. The most important thing is that in this game it is the time that matters (displayed in the top middle of the screen). You have to get through every level as quick as possible, and break the least number of eggs. Every broken egg eqals 10 penalty seconds. These seconds will be added to your total playing time. Try to pass the game with the shortest time possible, because at the end it will be placed in the highscores table.

Play Instructions:

Use arrows on the keyboard to move him left (left arrow) and right (right arrow). To drop an egg press the space bar, but first make sure that you are standing above the color-corresponding carriage.
