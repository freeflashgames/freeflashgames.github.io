Game Description:

Test your nerve as an Air Traffic Controller coping with mounting pressure and a constantly changing weather situation. When will you crack?

Play Instructions:

Click on an aircraft (square) and use the menu that appears to send the aircraft instructions. Aircraft circling the outside of the radar want to land - assign them a runway for landing from their menu. Aircraft at the departure gate want to take off - assign them a runway for takeoff from their menu. You will lose points while an aircraft flys through bad weather (the red patches on the radar). Aircraft may taxi through bad weather without losing points. When two aircraft collide - game over!
