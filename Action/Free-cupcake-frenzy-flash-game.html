Game Description:

In this game, you will need to create cupcakes for the customers. When a customer arrives, he or she will order a specific type of cupcake. You will then create the cupcake by first clicking the correct cupcake base, and then the cream or sauce, and then the toppings, and finally click the customer to give him the cupcake and collect the money. If you created the wrong cupcake the customer will not take it, and you will need to click the rubbish bin to dump it. At each level, you will need to earn a particular amount of money in order to proceed. As the game progresses there will be more and more customers and the game will be more and more difficult.
