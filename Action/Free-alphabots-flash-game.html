Chase after the Alphbots in this 3D action puzzle word game. Collect the longest words you can as quickly as possible in each level, across 3 different worlds.

Play Instructions:

Move around each level collecting up the Alphabots to make words. Longer words score more points. When you have collected a sequence of Alphabots that makes a word, press SPACEBAR.
