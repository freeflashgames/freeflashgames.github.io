Game Description:

Pressure Panic 2 is a quick action game, where walls are closing up on you from three sides. You can push them back by throwing bombs at them, and pick up masses of powerups which help you to survive as long as possible.

Play Instructions:

Move in the direction of a wall and press the mouse button to throw a bomb in that direction. Upon impact, it will push the wall back. When you are out of energy, stop throwing bombs for a while to regenerate it. Pick up powerups to help you. The walls move closer faster and faster the longer you live, so try to avoid touching them as long as possible. When you finally touch them (which ends the game) you can enter your score in a highscore list.
